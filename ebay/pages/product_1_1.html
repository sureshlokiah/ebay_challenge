    Great looking Rolex watch for any occasion.

    Beautiful watch with classic styling. It's not one of those in your face watches. Somewhat conservative look with black dial and bar indices with Arabic 3.6 and 9 for markers. It's very easy to read day or night. The 39mm size is very comfortable to wear and the combination polished and brushed stainless gives it that classic look. Fits perfectly on my 6 3/4" wrist. Explorer is supposed to be a sports watch but can easily be worn with a suit and not look out of place.
